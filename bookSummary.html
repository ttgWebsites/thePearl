Book Summary

  • So basically...
  • The Pearl is a novella about a Mexican-Indian pearl diver who finds an impossibly large pearl. Kino who had previously been a man of strong morals and virtues allows the pearl and the great wealth it offers him to corrupt and change him into a horrible man. The new Kino is far distant from the man he once was, and his newfound greed allows him to become careless husband and father. Ultimately the wealth presented is too much for Kino to handle and his life is never the same following the finding of the pearl. Steinbeck shows through his writing the problems greed can present in ones life, and just how much one can change when money is thrown into a persons life who previously had very little.
