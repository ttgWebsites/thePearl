Basic Info

  • Title
  • The Pearl
  • Author
  • John Steinbeck
  • Published
  • The Viking Press, 1947.
  • Genre
  • Parable or Allegory
  • Setting
  • The novel is set in a Mexican village known as La Paz on the west coast sometime during the late 19th century or early 20th century.
  • Point Of View
  • The narrator tells the story in the third person point of view, however he or she is able to dive into the characters thoughts and provide important details.
  • Conflict
  • The main conflict in the novel begins when Kino finds a very large and valuable pearl. When this happens he is suddenly faced with the possibility of being extremely wealthy and changing his and his families life forever. However greed of other people threatens Kino and his family, eventually leading to a tragic end.
